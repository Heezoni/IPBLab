Welcome to the Interactive Product Design Lab. IPD Lab. is a research lab affiliated to the Department of Design at UNIST. Our lab’s primary activity is to design and realize future interactive products through investigating aesthetic form & materials, along with the design of novel interactions. Our research converges product design, physical computing, and programming knowledge & skills in order to develop new interactive products for better user experience.
