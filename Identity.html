Identity

Our lab aims to propose methods and design research directions that blend design research with practice. Most of the previous examples of design studies connected to practice are mainly products made by intuitions from stylistic and experienced designers. On the other hand, interactive products in design studies mostly result in academic papers, patents, or exhibitions due to technical and structural design deficiencies
