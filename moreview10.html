SinKing:

The average of 6~7 dishes are used for a meal in a small family composed of 1~2 members. However, the minimum number of dishes to be washed at a once by the traditional compact dishwasher is over 50 dishes. Consequently, when small household use the traditional compact dishwasher, an enormous inefficiency of use is happen which causes waste of water and poor hygiene. To solve the problem, we suggest “SinKing”. ‘SinKing’ is a new dishwasher concept for small families such as newlyweds.
